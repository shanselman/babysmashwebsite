About BabySmash!

BabySmash! was created by Scott Hanselman for his young children, so they could enjoy banging on the keyboard without causing any damage to the computer.

As babies smash on the keyboard, colored shapes, letters, and numbers appear on the screen. BabySmash! locks out the Windows Key, Ctrl-Esc, and Alt-Tab so your baby can't exit the application accidentally.

This project is a loving homage to the excellent AlphaBaby for Macintosh. BabySmash! is completely free and open source!

Quick Tips

  • Exit: Press ALT-F4 to close the application
  • Options: Press Shift-Ctrl-Alt-O to open settings
